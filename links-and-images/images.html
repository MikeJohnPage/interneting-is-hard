Images

This page covers image formats. See linksfor info on links.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos

GIFs

GIF images are good for animations

PNGs

PNG images are good for logos, diagrams, and icons

SVGs

SVG images are amazing. Use them whenever you can.

JPGs, GIFs, and PNGs are all raster graphics. This means they use a bitmap (grid of pixels) to display the image. This means the images don't scale well. SVG's on the other hand are vector graphics, meaning they are defined in terms of points on a Cartesian plane. This means they scale up and down well.
